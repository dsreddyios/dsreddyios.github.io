Our Courses

In-depth, practical training across the generative AI landscape.

🤖

Generative AI Fundamentals

Build a solid foundation in generative AI concepts, models, and tools. Ideal for beginners and cross-functional teams.

  • Introduction to LLMs & transformer architecture
  • Prompt engineering basics & best practices
  • Ethics, bias, and responsible AI
  • Hands-on with ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
  • Capstone: Build a simple AI assistant
⚡

Advanced Prompt Engineering

Master techniques to elicit precise, high-quality outputs from generative models for complex tasks.

  • Chain-of-thought & few-shot prompting
  • Prompt chaining and decomposition
  • Role-based and persona prompting
  • Automated prompt optimization
  • Real-world case studies (code generation, analysis)
🔧

Fine-Tuning & Deployment

Learn to customize open-source models and deploy them in production environments.

  • Fine-tuning LLaMA, Mistral, and Phi
  • Parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA)
  • RLHF fundamentals and preference tuning
  • Model quantization and optimization
  • Deployment with Hugging Face, vLLM, and cloud APIs
🎨

Generative AI for Creatives

Harness AI for image, video, audio, and 3D content generation. Perfect for creative teams and content producers.

  • Image generation (Stable Diffusion, DALL·E, Midjourney)
  • Video synthesis (Runway, Pika, Sora)
  • Audio and music generation
  • 3D model generation
  • Workflow integration and prompt crafting for creatives

Comprehensive Curriculum

Key topics covered across our training programs.

Large Language Models (LLMs)

Architecture, training, fine-tuning, and applications of models like GPT, LLaMA, Claude, Gemini, and Mistral.

Image & Video Generation

Diffusion models (Stable Diffusion, DALL·E 2/3), GANs, and emerging video generation techniques.

Prompt Engineering

Crafting effective prompts, iterative refinement, advanced strategies, and evaluation metrics.

Model Fine-Tuning

Full fine-tuning, parameter-efficient methods, instruction tuning, and alignment techniques.

AI Ethics & Safety

Bias detection and mitigation, fairness, transparency, and responsible AI frameworks.

Deployment & Scaling

Serving models via APIs, containerization, orchestration, and monitoring in production.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Building knowledge-augmented systems with vector databases and external data sources.

AI Agents & Tool Use

Creating autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and use tools to accomplish tasks.
